Theoretically tumbling through troubled times,
My minds mentality, mortally mashed,
Righteous reality rapidly ravaged,
Whilst wishful, wonderment, wrongly warped.
Loving, longevity, loathingly lost,

The mind of a lost man,
Cradled by all that care,
Lost through choice,
Or that of despair?

An angel appears, announcing accountability and avoids
Certain criticisms, causing Christianity’s certain crumble.
Supreme symbols suddenly shattered, so
Mankind must malevolently manage morality
Individually. Initially I implicate irrevocability instantly.

Now aware of no other higher being,
He finds his feet,
And when he looks, he is truly seeing,
He swears never again to admit defeat.

Forever forgetting fundamental flaws,
Beings benefit blundering brutalities,
Divided difference’s dispels derogatory divulgement,
Personal preservation promises psychological paving,
Ever evolving essential empathy.

Again confused, he reaches for his gun,
Looks into the mirror,
But all he sees is his own son.
Suddenly aware of his prior decision,
He renters life, back to remission.
