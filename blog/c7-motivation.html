Motivation

How does life look through the 'pleasure, passion and purpose' lens?

Many believe in and seek the "3 P's" in the order they are displayed below.

  • Pleasure - short-term highs, living in the fast lane, live fast die young.
  • Passion - living in the ebb and flow of the universe, while not light speed, time has ceaced to exist here.
  • Purpose - unique to the individual and a source to boundless cosmic energy

While this way of life may be completely viable for some, it is fraught with inconsistency and can oft lead to an internal unbalance and subsequently yield less happiness than from first glance.
The happiness felt and gained from living a life of passion is far more sustainable ... but finding ones passion and flow is not always so easy. Purpose, but more specifically "Higher Purpose" if you can find it, is a place from which eternal happiness and endless drive can come from. As spoken about in this chapter, I have always found it easier to find/use my 'Higher Purpose' to fall into and feel my flow and then when I occasionally venture out of my norm to taste those momentary, intense emotions that are pleasure. I savour them all the more and their resonating effect lasts longer and puts spring in my step as I return to my flow.
